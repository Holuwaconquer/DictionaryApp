About LingoLookup

LingoLookup is a fast, lightweight online dictionary that helps you discover new words every time you visit. It gives you instant access to:

  • Definitions and parts of speech
  • Semantic meanings
  • Word origins
  • Pronunciation with audio

Whether you're learning English, expanding your vocabulary, or just curious, LingoLookup is free, offline-capable (PWA), and built with simplicity in mind.
